Ebook

Electronic books have more appearances than printed, which depend on cryptographic devices, programs, and databases that provide reading and access, the formats in which the books are delivered and, finally, the content elements themselfs.

Jeff Gomez is a representative of authors who declare "death to" printed books. It is thought that they donot have a future because they will be squeezed out by electronic texts, but the publisher's role is still considered unapproved. List of five reasons why publishers will survive in the digital age:
  • they will find talented authors because the publisher's job is to disclose texts worth reading
  • they will be supported by gifted authors, because "even the genius needs the editor"
  • they will provide gifted authors with visibility and promotion of works in digital environment
  • they will sell works of art and, as they have done so far, will pay for the author's fees.
